Hal's Story

Hal has been an Entrepreneur for over 25 years. He owned and operated a Seminar Company where he organized and ran Leadership and Sales seminars in Canada and in the USA

In 2000, He sold that business and partnered with International Author and World-renowned speaker RobinSharma. As Vice President and General Manager at Sharma Leadership International, he handled all of Robin's speaking engagements, organized open and corporate workshops (worldwide), was responsible for client engagements and helped develop their Leadership Development & Training programs. As the company grew, he become CEO and Master Facilitator of "Lead Without a Title Global, Inc. This was a Train-the-Trainer program using a blended learning approach. In 3 short years, he certified over 200 Facilitators worldwide + ran the course for organizations such as Citigroup, Tim Horton’s, Rogers Communications, RBC and many other groups around the globe

He has worked with organizations across Canada, the USA and in the Middle East (Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Kuwait). He has conducted workshops in India, Turkey, Portugal, France, Italy, Egypt, Malaysia, South America and Switzerland. Today, Al is a Professional EOS Implementer where he uses his 25 years of experience as an Entrepreneur, Business Coach and Facilitator / Trainer to help Entrepreneurial Leadership teams get what they want from their Business
